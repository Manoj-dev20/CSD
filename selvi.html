B.E, M.E, PHD

The Department of Computer Science and Design (CSD) at RRCE, established in 2023, is a forward-thinking branch that blends computer science fundamentals with UI/UX, digital media, and human-computer interaction. Unlike traditional CS programs, CSD focuses on both technical and creative aspects, making graduates highly sought after in fields like software development, UI/UX design, AI-driven interfaces, game development, and interactive media.

Our state-of-the-art labs, including AI/ML labs, UI/UX design studios, and software development centers, provide hands-on experience with the latest industry tools. Choosing CSD ensures a versatile career path in emerging domains such as product design, full-stack development, AR/VR, and data visualization.

With a curriculum designed to meet industry demands, students graduate with a unique skill set that bridges the gap between technology and design, opening doors to top tech companies and research opportunities worldwide.
