About Us

Established in 2005, managed by Moonglimpse Charitable and Education Trust (MCET), Bangalore. The College is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, Govt. of Karnataka & affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum. The college has also been certified ISO 9001:2015 institution.
