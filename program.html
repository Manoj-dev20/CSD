Department Events and Programs

Empowered minds. Boundless curiosity.
From academic brilliance to creative triumphs,
our CSD students are shaping the future with passion and purpose.

Workshop On Prototype | Process Design and Development

March 27, 2025

On March 27, 2025, the Department organized a workshop on Prototype | Process Design and Development led by Mr. Lokesh, a mentor at IIC, RRGI, and founder and startup consultant at Agasthya Academy, Bangalore. The session provided students with valuable insights into the fundamentals of prototyping, process structuring, and product development strategies. It emphasized the importance of iterative design, MVP (Minimum Viable Product) creation, and product-market fit. Students were encouraged to think innovatively and gained a practical understanding of how early-stage ideas can evolve into scalable solutions. The workshop also served as a motivation for those interested in entrepreneurship and startup incubation.

Food Fest

November 19, 2024

The department hosted a vibrant Food Fest themed "Cooking Without Fire", where students showcased their culinary creativity by preparing innovative, fireless dishes. Held in a lively and collaborative environment, the event encouraged teamwork, presentation skills, and entrepreneurial thinking. Students not only prepared a variety of dishes but also sold them at reasonable prices, simulating a real-time food business experience. The fest was a delightful blend of taste, talent, and teamwork — offering a break from academics while fostering practical skills.

IOT Mega IDEATHON

December 10, 2024

On December 10, 2024, the Department of IoT organized the IOT Mega Ideathon — a dynamic hackathon designed to challenge and inspire young innovators. Participants worked in teams to develop creative IoT-based solutions addressing real-world problems. The event fostered rapid ideation, technical collaboration, and prototype development under time constraints. It provided a platform for students to showcase their problem-solving skills, apply interdisciplinary knowledge, and embrace the spirit of innovation through hands-on experience in emerging technologies.

Student Development Program

November 14-16, 2024

The department organized a Student Development Program titled "Enter the Gameverse", conducted by Mr. Dheeraj C., Assistant Professor at RRCE. The session immersed students into the world of game development, introducing them to popular game engines and the fundamentals of designing interactive experiences. Students explored the step-by-step process of creating games — from concept to code — and gained hands-on experience by building simple 2D games. The program not only sparked creativity but also provided a practical foundation for anyone curious about pursuing game design and development.

About Us

Established in 2006, managed by Moogambigai Charitable and Education Trust (MCET), Bangalore. The College is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, Govt. of Karnataka & affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum. The college has also been certified ISO 9001-2015 institution.
