Our Journey

The Computer Science and Design (CSD) department at RajaRajeswari College of Engineering (RRCE) was established in 2023 with an intake of 60 students. The department offers a Bachelor of Engineering program affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The curriculum integrates computer science fundamentals with design principles, preparing students for diverse roles in IT consultancy, programming, hardware engineering, network administration, animation, virtual reality, and game development. The department also houses a Center of Excellence in the Internet of Things (IoT), fostering hands-on learning and innovation. Students actively participate in co-curricular activities such as workshops, technical seminars, expert lectures, and industry visits, enhancing their practical skills and industry readiness.

About Us

Established in 2005, managed by Moonglimpse Charitable and Education Trust (MCET), Bangalore. The College is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi. College is Affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum. The college has also been certified ISO 9001:2015 certification.
