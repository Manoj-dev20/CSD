About Us

Established in 2006, managed by Moogambigai Charitable and Education Trust (MCET), Bangalore. The College is approved by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi, Govt. of Karnataka & affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum.
